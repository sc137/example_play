Playing with base64 encoding within html

I heard about pages being completely embedded within href's on a recent episode of Security Now and thought that would be fun to play with. Here are a couple of practical examples I derived from some google and this datatest page.

From the terminal, run this command to get your encoded files:

openssl base64 -in original_file -out encoded_file

This text test should provide a page that says "A brief note" but doesn't actually link to another page.
